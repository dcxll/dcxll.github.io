Jiangxi Hot Tea Products

Yunwu Tea

Grown in the misty mountains of Lushan, this tea has a fresh fragrance and mellow taste, with a history dating back to the Han Dynasty.

Gouguao Tea

Produced in Jingdezhen, it is famous for its tight shape, green color, and sweet aftertaste, favored by tea lovers since the Song Dynasty.

FuLiang Tea

A traditional famous tea in Jiangxi, with a unique orchid fragrance and clear soup, rich in antioxidants and nutritional value.

Wuyuan Tea

From the beautiful Wuyuan county, this tea has a tender leaf texture and a refreshing taste, known as "the pearl of Jiangxi tea".

Ninghong Tea

One of China's famous black teas, produced in Yichun. It has a reddish-brown color, a sweet aroma, and a smooth taste.
