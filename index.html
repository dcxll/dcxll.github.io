History of Jiangxi Tea

Famous western teas have a long history, laying the foundation stone in Han and Tang Dynasties, flourishing in Song and Ming Dynasties

well-known in Qing Dynasty. Famous teas such as Lushan Yunwu Tea, Dog Gunnao Tea and Ning Black Tea have won numerous awards,

at home and abroad, and their intangible skills have been passed down from generation to generation.
